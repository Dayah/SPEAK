Tennessee Citizens for Wilderness Planning
Dedicated to protecting natural lands and waters through public ownership, legislation, and cooperation with the private sector.

EarthFest 2002
The official site of Knoxville's Earth Day celebration.

UT Environmental Resources
This is an excellent resource to find classes with an envinonmental emphasis, professors, degree programs, and many other resources

Campus Committee on the Environment
Information and links about the greening process at UT.

Dogwood Alliance
An alliance of 60 citizen groups from Arkansas to North Carolina united to defend our native ecosystems against the highly-mechanized, unregulated arm of industrial forestry.

Foundation for Global Sustainability (FGS)
FGS is a multi-disciplinary advocacy and activist organization which monitors and addresses social and environmental issues in the Upper Tennessee Valley and the Southern Appalachian Mountains.

Hellbender Press
East Tennessee's eye on the Earth, Hellbender Press is a great, free newspaper covering local and regional environmental issues. Pick up a copy at you favorite hang-out (if they don't have it, you should think about hanging out elsewhere) or read the latest issue on the web.

Save Our Cumberland Mountains (SOCM)
A rural Tennessee citizen's organization working for social, environmental, and economic justice.

Student Environmental Action Coalition (SEAC)
A national student run network working to fight environmental injustices.

Tennessee Green
A plethora of links, news, and editorial about environmental issues in Tennessee

Sustainable Measures
A website that provides indicators as to the progress towards a sustainable economy, society and environment.

KUB's Green Power
KUB's information page on converting to a cleaner energy source.

Veggie Van
This is a cool site devoted to the subject of biodiesel!

Campus Ecology
Find out ways to make college campuses more green!
